Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with PlastixThinker?

Getting started is easy! Simply visit our dedicated chat page and start chatting with PlastixThinker, our AI environmental expert. You can also upload documents to get personalized insights.

What types of files can I upload?

We support PDF, TXT, DOC, and DOCX files. These can include research papers, articles, educational materials, or any documents related to microplastics and ocean conservation.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All uploaded documents are processed securely, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect your information. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I use PlastixThinker for educational purposes?

Absolutely! PlastixThinker is designed for educational use. Teachers, students, researchers, and anyone interested in learning about microplastics can benefit from our AI-powered platform.
